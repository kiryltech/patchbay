Imagine you have a toy box (computer memory).

Usually, when you're done playing with a toy, you put it back on the shelf so there's room for new toys.

A memory leak is when you take toys out but forget to put them back. Eventually, the toy box gets so full you can't fit anything else in it, and you can't play anymore!
